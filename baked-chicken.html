Tender Italian Baked Chicken

Description

Flavorful and moist baked chicken.

Chicken Breast coated with parmesan cheese, garlic powder, and Italian bread crumbs.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 3/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 3/4 cup Italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F
  2. In a bowl, mix the mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, and garlic powder.
  3. Place bread crumbs in a separate bowl.
  4. Dip chicken into the mayonnaise mixture, then into the bread crumbs to coat.
  5. Arrange coated chicken on a baking sheet.
  6. Bake 20 minutes in the preheated oven, or until chicken juices run clear and coating is golden brown.
